Workshop: Advanced Chakana - The Garden of Pachamama

Now is the time of gathering together all of us who are the Weavers of the Tapestry of Light. The time of waiting is over.

The Grid of the Kaypacha, the energetic realm of Pachamama, is like a garden in the physical world. The segues and saways are the rows in that garden. In order to have good, strong, healthy plants to grow, the soil must be healthy and clean. The gardener tills the ground, bringing up to the surface, everything that is dead or decayed, no longer living, so he/she can remove all that. Next, the gardener nutures the soil, feeding and watering it, so it can hold new seeds. The new seeds are planted and tended lovingly. Over time, those seeds grow into strong, healthy plants that can feed the community. Together, we till the Garden of Pachamama, working in the grid of the Kaypacha, removing what no longer feeds Life.

Together, we feel the joy of planting new Seeds of Life, nurturing them with Love, Light, and Delight.
